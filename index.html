About Us

House of Prayer Church For All Nations has been engaged in the movement of the Holy Spirit for several years and will continue the move centered on the faith of the cross and expand the Kingdom of God through the message of the Fivefold Gospel" and the Threefold Blessing".

Furthermore, HOPFAN, being a church that practices the serving the heart of Jesus Christ and doing His Mission until the Lord's second coming, seeks to become an example for the society.

In our services each week, you can expect powerful praise and worship that helps us get into and stay in the presence of God and a timely and practical message coming from the Word of God to Encourage, Equip and Challenge believers of all ages to Grow in the Lord, Live Exemplary lives, and Reachout to the World around us.

We are a Church that is Passionate about Spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ - Supporting and Planting local cjurches in our Nation and beyond, Serving our communities and neighborhoods in various ways, and Supporting local organizations that work with the vulnerable in our Nation.

Our Mission

HOPFAN has a God-ordained mandate to impact the world.

We are called to present the Gospel to the lost, disciple nations, feed the hungry, clothe the poor, minister to the elderly, widows and orphans, visit prisoners and reach out to people of all ages and all walks of life

God requires that we teach people how to apply biblicaltruth in every facet of their lives and encourage christians to influence every aspect of the world around them.

With the prayers and support of our friends and partners around the world, we present a creative and relevant message of Christ and His Love for people worldwide using the most powerful forms of media and communications available, as well as the development and distribution of practical teaching resources.

To provide National humaritarian aid to hunting people and teach Christians and non-Christians alike how to enjoy everyday life by applying biblical principles in all they do.

Mission

Reaching Out the World

House of Prayer For All Nations is a missions Church. From it's inception, the heart of Bishop Emily has been to build a church that will raise great men and women of God, who would go into the City, the Nation, the World to raise more great men and women. Bishop Emily has desired to see a Church that will Impact, in a very Practical and Real way, the Lives of the Communities and People around us.

The Vision of HoPFAN is to Win Souls and Make Disciples. We understand that this involves us taking various approaches to reach into and have an Impact in our families, communities, as well as train and equip belivers to be able to raise up others.

HoPFAN approaches missions in two streams - Home missions - within Kitale and Kenya; and international missions - primarily targeting several African countries.

Home Missions

This has meant reaching the city and it's environs foremost. The life of HoPFAN is found in it's cell groupd, where relationships are built and nurtured, believersencourage each other in the Walk of Faith. For this reason, the cell groups provides a unique platform to reach to friends, neighbors, relatives, work-mates and acquitances.

Home Missions also targets needs throught the country - including our annual Serve Day, Prisons outreaches, Mercy ministries, Hospital outreaches Prayers walks, Evangelism events among many more. These are done within the cell groups and corporately.

In addition, HoPFAN aims to reach the nation through planting of local congregations, as well as working with like-minded partner Churches around the Country. Beyond Kitale, we've had the privilege of planting HoPFAN Churches in Eldoret, Matunda, Kisumu

Our Work

Giving

Our Generous giving - through tithes, offerings, mission commitment and others mave made it posible for us to meet our needs of our growing church as well as doing everything God has called us to do in impacting our communities, our Country and Continent with the Love and Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Give

Financial giving in the Church is mainly in two categories - Tithes and Offerings. The Bible teaches us to honor God by giving 10% of our income (that's Tithe). Anything we give above that is an offering.

At HoPFAN, we have two categories of Giving that are cause - specific: Mission Commitment goes directly to our outreach endeavors. This Giving helps us to invest in our communities, support missionaries, plant churches and so much more. Secondly, Our Project Advance Giving helps position the Church for upgrade needs as they arise.

We believe that there is a Blessing that God gives to Us as we invest in the affairs of His Kingdom and so we love to give at House of Prayer For All Churches, Kenya, and have a deep Bible based value for every opportunity we have to Give.
